Company Profile

Technix India Solutions Private Limited, founded in 2004, delivering a wide spectrum of Software and IT Consulting services ranging from Product Development, Migration, Porting, Testing, Custom Software Development and Distribution of Software for Desktop, Cloud and Mobiles.

We have provided solutions in private and public sector which ranges from small scale industries to huge supply chains such as Civil Courts, Forest Department, Banks, Show Rooms, Shops, Manufacturing Companies, Retail outlets, Accountants, Supply chain etc.



OUR STORY

TECHNIX INDIA was started to help customers and business to save their rising IT cost without impacting the quality. We deliver end-to-end solutions through valuable strategy development, consulting services, and highly skilled engineering execution. We are known for our Quality, Delivery on Time, and Systematic Post-Sales Maintenance Services.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to provide customers a specialized, reliable, high-quality, sophisticated services with cost savings. Our Customers must experience that working with TECHNIX INDIA is a more professional, less risky way to develop and implement projects than working completely in-house.

CORE VALUES

Our aspiration is to recognize your business, share a common business goal with you, and help you reach your objectives. We value the long-term relationship we create with our customers and ultimately become an extension of their businesses.
